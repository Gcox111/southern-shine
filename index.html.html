Frequently asked questions

What to know before your appointment.

Do you come to my home or workplace?

Yes. Southern Shine is a mobile service. We travel throughout Central Mississippi and surrounding areas to approved service locations.

How long does a full detail take?

Timing depends on the vehicle size and condition. When you request an appointment, we can provide a more accurate time estimate.

Can you remove pet hair, sand, and stains?

Yes. Stain removal is $50, and excessive sand or pet hair removal starts at $30. Final pricing depends on the amount of work required.

Do you offer window tint?

Yes. Contact us with your vehicle year, make, model, and the windows you want tinted for availability and pricing.

Do you need access to water or electricity?

Requirements may vary by appointment and equipment setup. We will confirm any site needs before the scheduled service.

How do I book?

Call or text 601-650-6984, or complete the booking form on this page. The form opens a prepared text message with your details.
